Privacy Policy for Topic Manager

Last Updated: June 06, 2025

Topic Manager, developed by Olaniyi Aborisade, stores user data (e.g., chat topics, message bookmarks, custom titles, sidebar position, and theme preferences) locally on your device using browser.storage.local. This data is not collected, transmitted, or shared with external servers or third parties. The extension operates offline and does not access personal identifiable information beyond what is provided within the chat interface it organizes.

For questions or concerns, contact us at larneyge@gmail.com.

We may update this policy as the extension evolves. Check back periodically for changes.
